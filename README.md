## ✨ Sample Try-On Feature

The flagship feature of Fashion Hub:

1. **Browse** products and click the **👗 Try** button
2. Add **measurements** (chest, waist, hip), preferred color, and delivery address
3. **Place a Try-On Order** — sample delivered in **1–4 hours**
4. **Track your order** live with a timeline tracker
5. **Give feedback** after delivery:
   - ✅ Perfect Fit → Add to cart and buy
   - ✂️ Needs Alterations → We'll adjust it
   - ↩ Return Sample → No charge
